Services

Video production for brands,
artists and events.

ShotByDiallo creates cinematic video content in Montreal for businesses, musicians, weddings, events and social media campaigns.

01

Brands & Companies

Professional video content helps your business look serious, build trust and explain what you offer quickly. I create clean marketing videos for companies, entrepreneurs, products, services and personal brands.

  • Brand videos
  • Promotional videos
  • Social media reels
  • Product or service videos
  • Client testimonials
  • Corporate event recaps
02

Artists & Music Videos

For artists, visuals are part of the image. I create music videos, performance visuals and short-form content that support your release, your personality and your sound.

  • Music videos
  • Performance videos
  • Release promo clips
  • Behind-the-scenes content
  • Artist portraits
  • Short-form social content
03

Events & Weddings

Events are about emotion, energy and important moments. I capture weddings, concerts, corporate events and private celebrations with a cinematic and natural approach.

  • Wedding films
  • Event recap videos
  • Concert coverage
  • Corporate events
  • Private celebrations
  • Highlight reels

Social Media Content

Social platforms need content that catches attention fast. I create vertical videos, short edits and clean promotional clips made for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook and YouTube Shorts.

  • Instagram reels
  • TikTok videos
  • YouTube Shorts
  • Event teasers
  • Campaign clips
  • Content packages

Process

A simple process from idea to final delivery.

01

Discovery

We talk about your goal, your audience, the type of video you need and the best direction for the project.

02

Planning

We define the concept, location, shot list, schedule and key moments so the shoot is organized.

03

Production

I film the project with attention to composition, movement, lighting and the feeling of the final video.

04

Delivery

You receive polished video content ready to post, share or use in your campaign.
